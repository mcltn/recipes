---
layout: recipe
title: Scrambled Eggs with Toast
category: Breakfast
prep_time: 5 minutes
cook_time: 5 minutes
servings: 1
ingredients:
  - 2 eggs
  - 1 tbsp butter
  - Salt and pepper to taste
  - 2 slices bread
  - Jam or butter for toast (optional)
---

1. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add butter.

2. While the butter is melting, crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk together with salt and pepper.

3. Once the butter is melted and foamy, pour in the egg mixture.

4. Stir gently and continuously until the eggs are cooked to your liking, about 3-4 minutes.

5. Meanwhile, toast the bread to your preferred crispness.

6. Transfer scrambled eggs to a plate and serve alongside the toast.

7. Spread jam or butter on the toast and enjoy!

Perfect for a quick and delicious breakfast!
